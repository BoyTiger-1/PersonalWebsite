Solo build · JavaScript, Python, runs in the browser

AlphaLab

A Bloomberg-terminal-style quant research desk that runs entirely in your browser.

One self-contained HTML file, no server and no API keys. It packs 118 strategy modules across trend, momentum, stat-arb, volatility, factors, and machine learning, plus portfolio optimizers and a risk lab that replays real crises like 2008, COVID, and 2022. An autonomous researcher generates hypotheses, backtests them through a five-stage statistical gauntlet, and files every result into a knowledge base so it never retries a dead end. All of it runs live on real market data going back to 2000.

Wildfire Risk Prediction AI

Tells you how likely a wildfire is right where you are.

Decision-tree models trained on NASA and Kaggle geospatial data. I built and shipped the whole thing myself: pulling and cleaning the raw data, engineering features from temperature, humidity, wind, and vegetation, checking the model actually held up, and putting it behind a Flask app that maps risk by region. Around 50 to 100 people used it.
