Wildfire Risk Prediction AI

One honest question: how likely is a fire where I'm standing, right now?

BrailleVision

Read physical Braille with the phone already in your pocket.

No special hardware. BrailleVision reads embossed or handwritten Braille dots straight from a camera or a photo, converts them to English text, and speaks the result aloud. We built it for real pages, machine-embossed and handwritten alike, not just clean digital samples, and put it in the hands of 50+ users.
